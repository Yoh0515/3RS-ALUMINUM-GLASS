About Us

A general contractor offering glass and aluminum services, The 3R's Glass and Aluminum Installation Services was founded in 2016 and specializes in sliding windows, sliding doors, fixed windows, awning windows, casement windows, swing doors, f rameless doors, cabinets, mirrors, and other internal and external glass and aluminum works . We manage a variety of industrial and commercial projects in the Metro Manila region and surrounding areas. We have more than 30 years of experience in this sector. Our business is a leader in offering value-added glass and aluminum fabricator services to our clients by establishing a strong relationship with them throughout the construction process . By exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust by our excellent performance, we hope to establish enduring connections with our customers .
